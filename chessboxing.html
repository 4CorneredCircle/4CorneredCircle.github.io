What is Chessboxing???

Chessboxing is the weird and cool sport you've never heard of! Players alternate rounds of chess and boxing. There are up to 11 rounds, starting with a 4 minutes chess round followed by 2 minutes of boxing. Between rounds is a one minute break where players change their gear. The history of chessboxing is a weird one. It was started in 1992 by a cartoonist, and that's just the beginning! For more, visit this.
