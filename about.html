About

All about Squared Circle and me!

I love reading books. I do it ALL the time. So on this website, I decided to review books that I've read. I'll try my best to give honest, fair reviews with some plot elements and some overall critique.


Stuff You Should Know

Q: Do any of your reviews contain spoilers?
A: There will never be anything I consider spoilers in any of my reviews. I want to give everyone the chance to experience the books for themselves! If you are very concerned about spoilers, though, read the book first and then come back to see what I said about it!


Q: What are your favorite genres?
A: I read a lot of fantasy and science fiction. Look at my top books in specific genres on the Book Awards page.


Q: Who are you?
A: I am a teenage book enthusiast who also enjoys coding. I decided to combine those interests by making this website from scratch using GitHub Pages.


Q: Doesn't "Squared Circle" have boxing connections?
A: Surprisingly, yes! If you didn't know about this, the term Squared Circle is commonly used to refer to a boxing ring! I did not know that when I was making this website, though. I was referencing my username on GitHub, 4CorneredCircle! Anyways, you learn something new every day!
